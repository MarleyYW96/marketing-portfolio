About Me

Hi, I’m Marley Wells, a Charleston-based digital marketer who thrives at the intersection of creativity and data-driven strategy. My husband and I live in Park Circle, where we spend our free time near the water and exploring the local music and festival scene.

I started my career in 2019 in the B2B life sciences industry and quickly discovered how much I enjoy the complexity and nuance of this space. While my academic background is rooted in creativity—with a Bachelor’s in Publication Studies and a Master’s in Writing, Rhetoric, and Media from Clemson University, I found myself increasingly drawn to the analytical side of marketing. I taught myself tools like Google Analytics and Google Tag Manager, explored both on- and off-page SEO, and learned how to turn data into actionable marketing strategies.

I’m passionate about understanding how all the moving parts of marketing—creative content, technical infrastructure, and performance analytics—come together to drive real-world impact. I’m also a firm believer in rolling up my sleeves and figuring things out firsthand. In fact, this portfolio is a product of that mindset—built using Visual Studio Code and deployed through GitHub.

Feel free to explore my work, and don’t hesitate to reach out if something catches your eye!
